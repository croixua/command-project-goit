Ice cream with condensed milk and strawberries

Ingredients

  • 480 ml whipping cream;

  • 380 g of condensed milk;

  • 150-200 g strawberries.

Preparation

  • Beat the cold cream with a mixer until creamy. Add condensed milk and punch again until smooth.

  • Cut the strawberries into small pieces, place in the creamy mass and stir. Place ice cream in a wide dish, cover with plastic wrap or a lid and place in the freezer for 3-4 hours.
